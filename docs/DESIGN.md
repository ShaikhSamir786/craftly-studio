# Craftly Studio
# Design Specification Document

Version: 1.0  
Purpose: Visual design guidelines for AI design agents and UI/UX teams

---

# 1. Design Vision

## Brand

Craftly Studio

## Core Message

> Not another template website. A digital experience crafted for your business.

---

## Design Goal

Create a premium digital agency experience that feels:

- Custom-built
- Creative
- Modern
- Trustworthy
- Technical
- Human

The website should visually prove the brand promise:

"A website that was specially designed for you, not copied from a template."

---

# 2. Design Personality

The interface should feel like:

## A Premium Creative Studio

Not:

- Traditional IT company
- Cheap freelancer website
- Generic WordPress agency
- Template marketplace


The visitor should think:

> "These people care about design and quality."

---

# 3. Target Audience

Primary audience:

Small and medium business owners.

Industries:

- Manufacturing
- Healthcare
- Restaurants
- Hotels
- Real Estate
- Professional Services
- Education


Design should communicate:

- Reliability
- Business understanding
- Professional quality
- Long-term partnership

---

# 4. Visual Direction

## Overall Style

Style keywords:

- Premium
- Minimal
- Editorial
- Modern
- Creative technology
- Boutique agency


Reference feeling:

- High-end design studio
- Modern SaaS company
- Product design agency


---

# 5. Layout Philosophy

## Principle:

"Less sections, more impact."

Avoid:

- Overloaded pages
- Too many cards
- Repetitive layouts
- Generic feature grids


Prefer:

- Strong storytelling
- Large typography
- Visual hierarchy
- Meaningful whitespace


---

# 6. Hero Section Design

## Purpose

Immediately communicate differentiation.

## Required Message

Headline:

"Not another template website. A digital experience crafted for your business."


Supporting text:

"We design and develop custom websites that help businesses build trust, attract customers, and grow online."


---

## Hero Visual Direction

Use:

- Custom website mockups
- Browser previews
- Device showcases
- Design process visuals


Avoid:

- Generic office photos
- Stock images
- Random illustrations


The visual should show:

"Custom digital craftsmanship."

---

# 7. Typography System

## Typography Personality

Should feel:

- Confident
- Modern
- Premium


Recommended fonts:

Primary:

- Geist
- Inter
- Manrope
- Plus Jakarta Sans


---

## Scale

Hero:

Large statement typography


Sections:

Clear hierarchy


Body:

Highly readable


---

# 8. Color Direction

## Brand Colors

Use a modern premium palette.

Direction:

Primary:

Deep dark tones

Used for:

- Hero
- Footer
- Premium sections


Neutral:

Soft backgrounds

Used for:

- Content sections
- Cards


Accent:

Creative technology highlight

Used for:

- CTA
- Important elements


---

# 9. Component Design Language

## Cards

Cards should feel:

- Premium
- Clean
- Spacious


Avoid:

- Heavy borders
- Excessive shadows
- Small crowded content


---

## Buttons

Primary CTA:

Purpose:

Drive conversion.


Examples:

"Start Your Project"

"Get Free Website Audit"


Style:

- Strong contrast
- Rounded corners
- Clear hover state


---

# 10. Homepage Design Structure

## Section Order

```

Hero

↓

Trust Indicators

↓

Problem Statement

↓

Solution / Services

↓

Industries

↓

Portfolio

↓

Process

↓

Testimonials

↓

Final CTA

↓

Footer

```

---

# 11. Service Section

Design goal:

Show outcomes, not technical features.

Services:

## Custom Website Development

Message:

"Unique websites designed around your business."


## Local SEO

Message:

"Help customers discover your business."


## AI Automation

Message:

"Use intelligent systems to save time."


## Maintenance

Message:

"Keep your website secure and improving."

---

# 12. Portfolio Design

Portfolio should not look like a gallery.

Avoid:

Only:

- Screenshot
- Project title


Instead show:

```

Business Type

Problem

Solution

Result

```

Example:

```

Restaurant Website

Challenge:
No online ordering presence.

Solution:
Custom website with menu integration.

Result:
Improved customer inquiries.

```

---

# 13. Animation Guidelines

Animations should communicate quality.

Use:

- Smooth page transitions
- Fade-in sections
- Hover interactions
- Micro animations


Avoid:

- Excessive movement
- Distracting effects
- Long loading animations


Animation feeling:

"Premium product experience."

---

# 14. Responsive Design

## Mobile Priority

Mobile users should experience:

- Clear typography
- Easy navigation
- Large CTA buttons
- Fast loading


---

## Desktop

Use:

- Wide layouts
- Strong visuals
- Large sections
- Rich storytelling


---

# 15. Navigation Design

Navigation should be simple.

Structure:

```

Logo

Services

Portfolio

About

Blog

Contact

CTA Button

```

CTA:

"Start Your Project"

---

# 16. Admin Dashboard Design

The admin interface should feel:

- Professional
- Efficient
- Simple


Style:

Modern SaaS dashboard.


Include:

## Dashboard

Cards:

- Services
- Blog Posts
- Projects
- Leads


## Content Management

Tables:

- Search
- Filter
- Edit
- Delete


## Editors

Use:

- Clean forms
- Clear grouping
- Preview options

---

# 17. Empty States

Design should guide users.

Example:

No projects:

Message:

"No projects added yet."

Action:

"Create your first project."

---

# 18. Error States

Errors should be:

- Clear
- Human-friendly
- Actionable


Example:

"Unable to load content. Please try again."

---

# 19. Accessibility Requirements

All designs must support:

- WCAG guidelines
- Keyboard navigation
- Clear focus states
- Proper contrast
- Readable typography
- Screen readers


---

# 20. Do Not Use

Avoid:

❌ Generic agency templates

❌ Excessive gradients

❌ Overused glassmorphism

❌ Stock business images

❌ Too many animations

❌ Corporate blue-only designs

❌ Feature overload


---

# 21. Design Success Criteria

The final design should achieve:

✓ Looks custom-built  
✓ Feels premium  
✓ Builds trust quickly  
✓ Clearly explains services  
✓ Encourages visitors to contact  
✓ Represents a modern development studio  


---

# Final Design Statement

Craftly Studio should look like a small team of talented developers and designers who create thoughtful digital experiences — not a company selling ready-made templates.

Every visual decision should reinforce:

> "Your business is unique. Your website should be too."

---
