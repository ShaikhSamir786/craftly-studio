# 1. Project Overview

## Product Name

Craftly Studio

## Product Description

Craftly Studio is a modern digital agency platform designed to showcase custom website development, AI automation, SEO, and digital solutions.

The platform serves two purposes:

1. Generate qualified leads from businesses looking for digital solutions.
2. Provide an internal CMS system for managing website content without developer involvement.

The website is designed around the core positioning:

> "Not another template website. A digital experience crafted for your business."

Unlike traditional agency websites that focus on technical capabilities, Craftly Studio focuses on business outcomes, custom experiences, and long-term partnerships.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Many small and medium businesses struggle with:

- Generic template websites that do not represent their brand.
- Poor online visibility.
- Lack of trust from potential customers.
- Difficulty maintaining and updating their websites.
- Limited access to modern technologies like AI automation.

Most businesses need a digital partner who understands their goals rather than just a website vendor.

Craftly Studio solves this by providing custom-designed websites and digital growth solutions.
